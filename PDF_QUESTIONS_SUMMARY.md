**3 Questions:** Easy, Medium, Hard

### Topic 32: Number Before and After
**3 Questions:** Easy, Easy, Medium
- **Easy:** What is the even number after 16?
- **Easy:** What number comes before 800?

**3 Questions:** Easy, Medium, Hard
- **Easy:** You pay £1 for a 60p item. How much change?

**3 Questions:** Easy, Medium, Hard

- **Medium:** Anne has 245 books. Her brother has 50 books. How many books altogether?

All questions follow child-friendly teaching methods:
1. **Count Up Strategy:** For addition/subtraction
2. **Place Value Method:** Breaking numbers into hundreds, tens, ones
3. **Visual Thinking:** Encouraging mental models
